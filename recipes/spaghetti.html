Spaghetti Bolognese

Spaghetti Bolognese: the traditional pasta recipe from Italy

Everyone loves pasta and its endless combinations, even if nothing beats the most classic pasta recipes. Spaghetti Bologneseis a classic Italian pasa recipe everyone likes. The Bolognese sauce is rich, thick, and flavorful

Thi easy recipe is made of vegetable stir-fry, meat and tomato sauce, together with herbs and spices that give it a winning flavor. Let it simmer for a while and share this tasty dish with your family at dinner.

Ingredients

  • Veal stifle, minced
  • 1 onion
  • Tomato sauce
  • oregano
  • Olive Oil
  • Lean Pork, minced
  • 1 Carrot
  • White Wine
  • Milk
  • Basil Leaves
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  1. First, peel the onion and carrot, them chop them finely. Pour a little oil into the pot and set it over medium-low heat
  2. When the oil is hot, add the onion and the carrot together with the salt and cook them for about 10 minutes or until tender and barely brown
  3. Add the minced meat and mix it using a spatual so that it does not stick together in big ieces. Add the salt, freshly ground black pepper and oregano to taste
  4. When the meat is no longer pink, increase the heat tp high, add the white wine and wait for the alcohol to evaporate, it will only take 2-3 minutes
  5. Lower the heat, then add the tomato sauce and the milk. Let the sauce simmer for 20 minutes
  6. When the bolognese sauce is almost ready, add a teaspoon of salt and the spaghetti, and as soon as the water startss boiling again start counting the minutes indicated on the package.
  7. When the spaghetti is ready, add the drained spagehetti to the bolognese sauce pot with the fire off, mix well.
