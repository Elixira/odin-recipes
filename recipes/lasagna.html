Cheesy Four-Cheese Lasagna

This four chesse lasagne is a stable meal for and family gatherings. Between each supple layer is delicious cheese a meat.

This no-bake skillet lasagna is made right on your stoveop and is a fast and easy alternative to store-bought hamburger mixes!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 28oz jar spaggetti sauce 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups dried mafalda noodles
  • 1 cup shredded mozzerella cheese

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. cook and stir beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. Add spaghetti sauce, tomatoes, onion, garlic, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Cook over low heat until sauce is hot, about 15 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, fill a large pot with lightly salted water to bring to a rolling boil. Cood mafalda noodles at boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain
  3. Add cooked and drained noodles to the sauce and stir until completely coated. Sprinkle mozzerella cheese on top.
  4. Set an oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
  5. Place skillet under the hot broil and cook until cheese is golden and bubbly, 3 to 5 minutes
